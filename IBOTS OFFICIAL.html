About Us

The iBots is an FTC robotics home team based in Wildwood, Missouri. We have eleven members who are extremely passionate about science, technology, engineering, and math. In this website, you can find our TeamFinder, our program that helps rookie teams or aspiring FTC members find local teams to join or shadow. You can also find our YouTube channel, where you can follow us on our journey our robot vlogs. Thank you for stopping by.
